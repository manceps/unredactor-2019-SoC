About Unredactor

Unredactor is an interactive app to edit with words. You can redact and enter text with this new app. The app is made to interact with the text and redact words etc. Not only that, this app is able to predict the words the user can come up with, and is enjoyable. The app is created for users to fill in the blanks, with the app being able to redact and predict words that are reasonable with the context and not unexpected like "Mad Libs." The Unredactor works by using a Google BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) model. The Unredactor app is a great way to show others the beauty and mystics of technology working on its own. Users will see the technology really working in the app.

Manceps Summer of Code

Manceps Summer of Code is an internship that was offered to a group of students from both Camas High School and Discovery High School from the company, Manceps, Inc. In this Summer of Code internship, this ambitious group work together and individually on a given task and share with the mentors about their progress daily. The students organized each other based on the previous knowledge they know to practice and excel in their given role. This summer for the students was busy as many hours were put into the creation of this app. The mentors provided many sources, tutorials, and websites to help the students on their given task. They helped the students in any way they needed and with the new information taught, they were able to execute their product. Below are our contributors in the creation of this app: Unredactor.
